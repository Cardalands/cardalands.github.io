CARDALAND

OPEN WORLD SPACE EXPLORATION NFT ADVENTURE GAME


PROLOGUE

Earth has discovered a new habitable planet. They observed it to find an Earth-like atmosphere with floating islands and name it Cardaland. Cardaland Enterprise launches a space program for citizens to participate in the first landing on Cardaland after building a colony by space rovers. The journey was short-lived and 21 ships that left earth, crash to an unknown nearby planet. With limited resources and unknown possible hostile alien threats outside the ship. The survivors were advised to stay in the ship until rescue arrived from the earth. Join The Community On Discord.
